Education is for self-reliance, so we should all strive for it. It is only through Education that development will be achieved. Bunagana Town Council is a small Town in Kisoro District in South Western Uganda bordering Democratic Republic of Congo (D.R.C) and Rwanda. It is about 500 km from Kampala the Capital City of Uganda. Bunagana Town Council is small and densely populated. It has an area of 10 square kilometers and population of 10,478 as per Census of 2013 making 1047.8 per square Kilometer. Today the population is over 28,000 people. Many people have problems among which poverty is the major one. The only solution to problems is education and without it, there is little hope for the future. The only lasting solution to the peoples' problems is to put Education in the first place. Due to this problem, when old people die, they leave their young ones suffering more without any thing;.

21st jan, 2024 MOTTO AND MISSION

All these cannot be achieved unless these are:

(I) Personal development – intellectual and spiritual development of the learners. (II) Vocational preparation – practical skills and character qualities to all the learners (III) Social training – initiating young people into society at local and National levels. The school is mandated therefore to: (a) Help each individual learner discover a philosophy in which he himself has significance. (b) Fit the young in a situation to prepare and cope with the future, By helping them to develop some useful and marketable skills that will be of use to others and ensure him or her employment (c) Introduce the learner to an established society so that he or she learns more about science and the world around him.!

NAME OF THE SCHOOL

BLESSED FUTURE PRE & PRIMARY SCHOOL,

The name of the school is Blessed Future Pre- and Primary school. It is under Mr. Kwizera Emmanuel, Mr. Nkurunziza Valence and Miss. Kabanyana Renatha, who are mandated to carry out Educational service throughout the country. It started on 28th January 2016.!

LOCATION

The school is located along Kisoro-Bunagana road,

The school is located along Kisoro-Bunagana road, after Mubuga Trading Centre, Bukara Village, Maziba ward, Bunagana Town Council, Kisoro District. It is a catchment area with access to pupils from within the Town council and its neighboring sub-counties as well as international boarders of Congo and Rwanda.

COMPOSITION

The school is a day mixed Pre-and Primary school

The school is a day mixed Pre-and Primary school, with initial enrolment of 396 pupils (251 boys and 145 girls) from nursery class to Primary 7. The school has inadequate classrooms with temporary structure with a hope to construct permanent classrooms in future. Boarding facilities are also being prepared!

OWNERSHIP

The school is privately owned

The school is privately owned by Mr. Kwizera Emmanuel, Mr. Nkurunziza Valence and Miss. Kabanyana Renatha. The school has enough land for future expansion. The process of registering the school is smoothly going on with all stake holders and the Ministry of Education and sports, and in few months to come, it will be registered by the government. Currently we are operating on provisional license.!

ORGANISATION AND MANAGEMENT

The school is run by teaching and non-teaching staff

It has a Head teacher and other ten teaching staff and four non-teaching staff, which see to it that it runs smoothly on daily, weekly, monthly and termly basis. The school has also a School Management Committee of 15 members (men and women) who oversee all the activities of the school and advise where necessary.!
