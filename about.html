Read about school profile

1- NAME OF THE SCHOOL The name of the school is Blessed Future Pre- and Primary school. It is under Mr. Kwizera Emmanuel, Mr. Nkurunziza Valence and Miss. Kabanyana Renatha, who are mandated to carry out Educational service throughout the country. It started on 28th January 2016. 2- LOCATION The school is located along Kisoro-Bunagana road, after Mubuga Trading Centre, Bukara Village, Maziba ward, Bunagana Town Council, Kisoro District. It is a catchment area with access to pupils from within the Town council and its neighboring sub-counties as well as international boarders of Congo and Rwanda. 3- COMPOSITION The school is a day mixed Pre-and Primary school, with initial enrolment of 396 pupils (251 boys and 145 girls) from nursery class to Primary 7. The school has inadequate classrooms with temporary structure with a hope to construct permanent classrooms in future. Boarding facilities are also being prepared 4- OWNERSHIP The school is privately owned by Mr. Kwizera Emmanuel, Mr. Nkurunziza Valence and Miss. Kabanyana Renatha. The school has enough land for future expansion. The process of registering the school is smoothly going on with all stake holders and the Ministry of Education and sports, and in few months to come, it will be registered by the government. Currently we are operating on provisional license.
